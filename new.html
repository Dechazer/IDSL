Our Core Programs

Transforming communities through sustainable development, climate action, and social empowerment across Kenya

01

Sustainable Agriculture & Food Security

Goal: Empower farmers with resilient, eco-friendly practices.

Key Activities

  • Promote agroecology (crop diversity, organic inputs, waste recycling)
  • Train farmers in soil erosion control & water conservation
  • Strengthen seed sovereignty & local food production
  • Reduce food loss & post-harvest waste
02

Climate Action & Green Innovations

Goal: Combat climate change through adaptation & mitigation.

Key Activities

  • Advocate for renewable energy (solar, biogas)
  • Implement carbon sequestration (agroforestry, no-till farming)
  • Support climate-smart livestock & fisheries
  • Train communities in disaster risk reduction
03

Humanitarian & Social Development

Goal: Improve livelihoods in crisis-affected areas.

Key Activities

  • Emergency food & water aid in droughts/floods
  • WASH programs (clean water access, sanitation)
  • Gender equality & anti-GBV initiatives
  • Peacebuilding & conflict resolution in pastoral regions
04

Food Systems Transformation & Advocacy

Goal: Influence policies for a fairer, healthier food future.

Key Activities

  • Promote indigenous crops & traditional diets for better nutrition
  • Campaign against harmful agrochemicals & GMOs
  • Lobby for pro-farmer policies & land rights
  • Document & protect indigenous knowledge (ITK)

Together, we can create a Kenya where no one goes hungry, nature thrives, and communities prosper.
